Farmstand Collection

Weekly harvest boxes, bespoke fruit crates, goat dairy, honey infusions, and aquaculture proteins—packed to travel or delivered to your Hawassa address.

Ask about chef partnerships and wholesale sourcing for restaurants or boutique grocers.

Ecolodge Residences

Minimal villas with panoramic decks, soaking tubs, and farm-to-table tasting menus. Morning meditations, guided farm walks, and chef dinners can be added on.

Ideal for restorative weekends, founder residencies, or small family getaways.

Event & Workshop Space

Convertible indoor / outdoor venues with AV, culinary lab, and breakout nooks under the trees. Host retreats, strategy summits, culinary pop-ups, or community dinners.

On-site planning support, farm experiences, and catering can be layered in.

Learning & Research Lab

Immersive practicums for universities, technical schools, and youth programs that combine regenerative theory with revenue-backed field experience.

Internships, research residencies, and partnership projects ensure education, innovation, and community impact stay funded by the farm’s core business.
